About Us

Hi, I'm Cuong, the founder of review travel. This is a website that helps you keep track of famous places through reviews by travelers, through which you will see their interesting points. Because these are the real experiences of tourists, the reviews will reflect the details and real fun of that place. You can view and leave a comment under the review, dropping the heart of the article is also a way to thank the person who created the review.

In addition, you can leave your information and gmail below the contact section if you want to leave a review of a certain place, then we will receive and consider to put it on the website. Thank you very much!
